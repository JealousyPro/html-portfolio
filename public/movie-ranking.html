The Best Movies According to Itachi Uchiha

Itachi's top 3 Anime of all-time.

Death Note

With its psychological thriller elements, moral dilemmas, and intellectual battles, this anime series would likely appeal to Itachi's analytical mind and love for strategic thinking.

Grave of the Fireflies

As a powerful and emotionally charged drama set during World War II, this Studio Ghibli film could have resonated with Itachi's capacity for deep emotions and his understanding of the human cost of war.

Ghost in the Shell

A cyberpunk anime franchise that delves into philosophical themes, futuristic technology, and complex moral questions, exploring the boundaries between humanity and artificial intelligence.
